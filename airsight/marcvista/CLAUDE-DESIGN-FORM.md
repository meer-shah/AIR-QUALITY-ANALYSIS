# Paste-into-Claude-Design text — MarcVista

Upload this `design-system/` folder into Claude Design's "Set up your design system" flow, then paste the two blocks below.

## Field 1 — Company name and blurb

MarcVista: a 2-week brand-and-product positioning sprint for early-stage tech founders. We get you crystal-clear on who you're building for and why you're different — combining brand strategy and product direction in one engagement — then help build the website and product direction around that clarity. Co-founded by Shahmeer. Surfaces we design for: marketing website, landing pages, pitch decks, LinkedIn + Instagram carousels, email, infographics, ads.

## Field 2 — Any other notes?

Load design-system/CLAUDE.md first — it sets the read order (BRAND-SUMMARY → voice → the right application file → tokens → components). Core message, anchor everything to it: "We clarify your brand identity first, then build your product around it." Archetype: The Mentor. Traits: thoughtful, practical, honest, founder-fluent.

Visual non-negotiables (these override any styling defaults):
• Primary color is exactly #7C3AED (violet) — the only brand color. Background is pure white #FFFFFF for marketing; #F5F5F5 is a panel tint only. Inter is the only typeface at every size. Text is pure black #000000.
• Radii: sharp 0px everywhere — the signature. No pill CTAs, no rounded marketing cards. Only avatars/status dots are round.
• No gradients-over-everything, no glassmorphism, no glow, no 3D, no generic "AI" visuals (orbs, neural meshes, wireframe globes). Lucide icons only, 1.5 stroke. Feature-icon chip = 40×40 brand/100 with a brand/600 icon.
• Imagery: abstract/geometric, violet on white or black (see the real banner in assets/templates/banners/). Never stock people, never AI-generated humans.

Voice & copy non-negotiables:
• Lead with the founder's problem, never with "we." Speak to "you." Short, active, direct. Confident but humble.
• Banned hype: revolutionary, game-changing, 10x, supercharge, unleash, leverage, synergy, seamless, stunning, "AI-powered" (bare). Banned agency-speak: brand architecture, strategic narrative, visual identity system.
• CTAs are sharp and specific ("Book a free positioning call") — never with an exclamation mark. Every claim earns a number or a name (2 weeks, $500K–$1.2M seed, 5+ years, 100K+ coding hours).
• Lead with the pillar that matches the founder's pain: wasted runway (pre-product), sales/fundraising (post-launch), or founder confidence (first-timer).

Everything should make an early-stage founder feel that MarcVista has done this before, sees what they can't, and will tell them the truth — not sold to.
