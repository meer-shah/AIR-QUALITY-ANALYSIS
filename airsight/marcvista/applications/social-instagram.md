# Instagram

Secondary channel. Same visual system as LinkedIn, slightly more visual-first. Founder register for captions, MarcVista register for carousels that teach.

## Aspect ratios

- **Feed:** 4:5 (1080×1350) default — most feed real estate. 1:1 (1080×1080) for grids.
- **Stories / Reels:** 9:16 (1080×1920).

## Carousel structure

- **Cover (hook):** one sharp line, violet accent word, mark in corner. Black or `brand/900`.
- **Thread (3–7):** one idea per slide, feature-icon chip + short headline + a line. White slides.
- **Payoff + CTA:** the takeaway, then "Follow @marcvista" / "Link in bio". Black or violet.
- Add a subtle "swipe →" affordance on the cover.

## Design rules

- Instagram type scale runs larger: cover headline 56–64px, body 24–28px (legible at thumbnail size).
- Sharp 0px corners, Inter, one violet accent per slide.
- Logo placement: small mark, consistent corner across the set.
- Color rotation: alternate paper interior slides with the occasional `brand/900` slide for emphasis — don't make every slide violet.

## Post types

- **Single-image spotlight** — one shipped result or one bold line on paper/violet.
- **Quote card** — a sharp opinion ("A mid product with sharp branding beats a superior product. Every time.") in display weight, mark in corner.
- **Number card** — one stat, huge, violet, one line of context.

## Stories / Reels

- **Cover:** mark + 4-word hook, `brand/900`.
- **First frame** of a Reel earns the watch in 1 second — open with the claim, not an intro.
- **Caption style:** Founder register, short, one idea, ≤2 hashtags.

## What we never post

Stock photos of people · meme templates as brand posts · rainbow gradients · 10-hashtag blocks · Title Case headline graphics · anything that looks like a generic "AI startup" template. No emoji on the *graphics* (captions may use one, sparingly, in Founder register).

## Caption template

```
[Hook line — the claim or the funny-but-true setup.]

[2–4 short lines. One idea. A real point or number.]

[CTA: Book a free positioning call — link in bio.]

#brand #startups #founders   ← max 3
```
