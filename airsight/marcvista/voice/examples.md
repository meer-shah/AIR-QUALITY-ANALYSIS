# Voice — do / don't by surface

Every pair shows the same message written wrong (hype/vague) then right (specific/MarcVista). Marketing surfaces use the MarcVista register; LinkedIn/IG openers show the Founder register.

## Homepage hero (headline)
- ❌ "Revolutionary AI-powered solutions to supercharge your startup."
- ✅ "A brand-first tech studio. We clarify your identity, then build around it."

## Homepage subcopy
- ❌ "We leverage cutting-edge technology to deliver world-class digital experiences."
- ✅ "In the age of AI, anyone can build software. The founders who win are the ones the market remembers. We make sure that's you."

## Section eyebrow (overline)
- ❌ "OUR INNOVATIVE SOLUTIONS"
- ✅ "FOR EARLY-STAGE FOUNDERS"

## Feature card
- ❌ "Seamless end-to-end synergy across your entire stack."
- ✅ "Discovery, design, engineering, and deployment under one roof. One team, zero handoffs."

## Primary CTA
- ❌ "Get Started Now →"
- ✅ "Book a free positioning call"

## Pricing / engagement headline
- ❌ "Flexible pricing for every stage of your journey."
- ✅ "Start with a positioning sprint. We take it as far as you need."

## Case study opener
- ❌ "We partnered with an industry-leading client to transform their business."
- ✅ "SuperFi needed their product shipped on a hard deadline. We delivered on track, then kept iterating on every piece of feedback."

## Email subject line
- ❌ "Unlock the full potential of your brand! 🚀"
- ✅ "Your brand vs. your product — which is doing the talking?"

## Email opening line
- ❌ "We hope this email finds you well. At MarcVista, we believe…"
- ✅ "Quick one: does your landing page sound like the same company as your product? Most founders' don't."

## LinkedIn post opener (Founder register)
- ❌ "Excited to share that MarcVista is revolutionizing the way founders build brands! 🚀"
- ✅ "In the era of polymaths, I'll call myself a generalist. Started in software. Then AI grabbed me. Then I got lazy and built agents to do the boring parts — and they worked."

## Instagram caption opener (Founder register)
- ❌ "Check out our amazing new project! Link in bio 🔥🔥"
- ✅ "A mid product with sharp branding beats a superior product with fuzzy branding. Every time. Here's why ↓"

## Error message
- ❌ "Oops! Something went wrong 😬"
- ✅ "That didn't go through. Refresh and try again — if it sticks, email info@marcvista.com."

## 404 page
- ❌ "Houston, we have a problem!"
- ✅ "This page doesn't exist. Back to the homepage, or book a call."

## About page
- ❌ "MarcVista is a passionate team of innovators dedicated to excellence."
- ✅ "We hired two teams once, sat in the middle translating between them, and lost time at every handoff. So we built the studio we wished we'd had: one team, one roof."

## Hiring post (Founder register)
- ❌ "Join our rockstar ninja team and disrupt the industry!"
- ✅ "We're hiring engineers who are AI-fluent and allergic to handoffs. You'll ship real product for founders, fast. No ramp-up theater."

## Anti-pattern checklist (reject copy that does any of these)
- [ ] Uses a banned hype word (revolutionary, supercharge, seamless, elite, stunning, AI-powered…).
- [ ] Makes a claim with no number or name behind it.
- [ ] Talks about "users/clients" in third person on a marketing surface instead of "you."
- [ ] Title Cases a headline, or uses ALL CAPS outside a ≤3-word overline.
- [ ] Leads with the technology/model instead of the founder's outcome.
- [ ] Has an exclamation point on a MarcVista marketing surface.
- [ ] Founder-register copy that's "funny" but never reaches a real point.
