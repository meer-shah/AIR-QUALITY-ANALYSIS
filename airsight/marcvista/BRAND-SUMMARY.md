# MarcVista — brand summary

## What it is
A **brand-and-product positioning sprint** for early-stage tech founders. In **2 weeks**, MarcVista gives a founder clarity on who they're building for and why they're different — combining brand strategy and product direction — then helps them build everything around that clarity. Co-founded by Shahmeer.

## Core message (never deviate)
**We clarify your brand identity first, then build your product around it.**

## Why it exists
In the AI era, anyone can build software — so the product is no longer the hard part. The difference-maker is clarity of the brand around it. MarcVista saves founders from the most expensive startup mistake: building the right product for the wrong audience, or the right product with the wrong story.

## Positioning (where it sits)
- Loud ↔ **quiet-confident** — confident but humble.
- Agency ↔ **practitioner** — a founder who lived it, not an agency selling frameworks.
- Theoretical ↔ **decisive** — hands you decisions, not a framework doc.
- Generic ↔ **specific** — every engagement is specific; numbers and names.

## Archetype / anti-archetype
**The Mentor** — a trusted guide who's walked the path and sees what the founder can't yet see. **Anti:** flashy, corporate, theoretical, generic.

## Personality — 4 core traits
**Thoughtful · Practical · Honest · Founder-Fluent.**

## ICP
1. **Pre-product founders** (just raised $500K–$1.2M seed, unclear positioning, $3K–$8K budget).
2. **Post-launch founders** (weak/generic messaging, prepping Series A or stuck at a ceiling).

## Messaging pillars
1. **Avoid wasted runway** (pre-product) · 2. **Accelerate sales & fundraising** (post-launch) · 3. **Founder confidence** (first-timers). Lead with the pillar that matches their pain; always return to the core message.

## The one-sentence promise
We get you crystal-clear on positioning before you build — so you ship the right product to the right market, faster.

## Tagline
**A brand-first tech studio.** / "Clarity before code." / "Brand sharp. Ship fast."

## Visual foundations snapshot
- **Color:** one primary — violet `#7C3AED` — over pure-white paper, `#F5F5F5` panels, pure-black text, Tailwind-gray neutrals. *(From the live site — the real visual identity. The brand book's color codes are superseded by this.)*
- **Type:** Inter only. Headlines 700, sentence case.
- **Radius:** sharp 0px — the signature.
- **Logo:** official angular two-stroke violet M/V monogram (`logo/mark.svg`).
- **Imagery:** abstract/geometric, violet on white or black (see the real banner in `assets/templates/banners/`). No stock people, no AI clichés.
- **Motion:** minimal — fade + small translate, ease-out.

## Voice in one paragraph
The Mentor: thoughtful, practical, honest, founder-fluent. Plain English, short active sentences, speak to "you," confident but humble. Lead with the founder's problem, never with "we." Every claim earns a number or a name; no hype, no agency-speak ("brand architecture," "strategic narrative"). A second register — Founder/LinkedIn (Shahmeer) — is witty and first-person; it lives in `shahmeer-design-system/`.

## What MarcVista wants you to feel in 3 seconds
*"This person has done this before, sees what I can't, and will tell me the truth about my brand."* Clear and trustworthy — not flashy.
