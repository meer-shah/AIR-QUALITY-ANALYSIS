# Vocabulary

The words MarcVista reaches for, and the ones it refuses. Pulled from the brand book's voice rules and do/don'ts.

## Words to prefer

**About us:** positioning clarity · brand-and-product sprint · clarity before code · 2-week sprint · we've been there · brand-first · the right product for the right market.

**About the founder (you):** you · your positioning · your runway · your customer · your launch · who you're building for · why you're different · your north star · your pitch.

**About outcomes:** clear · crystal-clear · clarity · positioning · close rate · differentiation · confidence · pivots prevented · runway saved · ship the right thing.

## Banned — hype

revolutionary, game-changing, 10x, cutting-edge, supercharge, unleash, leverage (verb), transform, synergy, seamless, robust, world-class, stunning, elite, next-gen, disruptive, **AI-powered** (bare adjective).

## Banned — agency-speak (brand book says don't)

**brand architecture · strategic narrative · visual identity system · strategic brand architecture optimization · brand activation · holistic · paradigm.** Say the plain-English version instead ("positioning clarity," "how you sound," "your logo and colors").

## Copy do / don't (from the brand book)

| DO | DON'T |
| --- | --- |
| Lead with *their* problem, not our solution | Open with "we" or "Here at MarcVista" |
| Use "you" — every sentence about the founder | Us-centric language that makes MarcVista the hero |
| Be specific — numbers, scenarios, before/after | "We help you grow" (vague) |
| Short sentences — scannability is respect | Long paragraphs that bury the point |
| One clear action per message | End with no next step |
| Acknowledge alternatives | Pretend we're the only option |
| Focus on outcomes (what changes for them) | Focus on features ("we do a 2-week sprint" isn't the point) |

## Verbs MarcVista uses

clarify · position · diagnose · sharpen · cut · decide · ship · build (around clarity) · prove · win.

## Verbs MarcVista avoids

leverage · utilize · empower · revolutionize · disrupt · supercharge · ideate · synergize · optimize (as filler).

## Numbers the brand likes

**2 weeks** (the sprint), **$500K–$1.2M** (ICP seed range), **$3K–$8K** (engagement), **3–6 months** (what agencies waste), and concrete web proof: **5+ years**, **100K+ coding hours**, named clients (NextRoll, SuperFi, Great Colorado Homes, Owiwi, Edfundo). Numerals, never vague "many/tons."

> ⚠️ Proof note: the brand book also references "20+ years of pattern recognition" / "dozens of founders" as framing. Lead with the concrete, defensible web numbers (5+ yrs, 100K+ hours, named clients) on public surfaces unless a specific stat is verified.

## Headline recipe

`[Their problem or a sharp positioning truth], [the clarity we bring].`
- "You can build the product. Can the market tell what it's for?"
- "Most founders build for the wrong market. We get you clear in 2 weeks."
- "Clarity before code. It's the cheapest mistake to fix and the most expensive to skip."
