# Brand

> Source of truth for this doc: the official **MarcVista Brand Book & Positioning Guide** (2024 edition, co-founded by Shahmeer). Visual identity (violet/Inter/sharp) comes from the live site; everything strategic below comes from the brand book.

## What MarcVista is

A **brand-and-product positioning sprint** for early-stage tech founders. In **2 weeks**, MarcVista gives a founder clarity on who they're building for and why they're different — combining brand strategy *and* product direction in one engagement — then helps them build everything (brand, website, product direction) around that clarity. The core message, used everywhere, is:

> **We clarify your brand identity first, then build your product around it.**

## Origin story

Shahmeer co-founded MarcVista after living the same frustration that plagues thousands of founders: you can build the product — everyone can now. In the age of AI, software is no longer the hard part. The hard part is knowing *what* to build, *who* it's for, and *how* to position it so the right people immediately understand why they need it. After watching founders (himself included) burn time and money building products nobody could clearly explain, he saw the root cause was almost never the product — it was the lack of brand clarity *before a single line of code*.

**The insight:** In the AI era, anyone can build software. The founders who win are the ones who can brand it clearly. Clarity before code isn't a luxury — it's the highest-leverage investment a founder makes.

## Why we exist

Because when anyone can spin up a product in a weekend, the difference-maker is no longer the product — it's the clarity of the brand around it. We help founders discover what they're actually solving, who they're truly solving it for, and how to position it so the right customers recognize it immediately. Then we help them build around that clarity.

## Positioning statement (formal)

Early-stage tech founders who have raised funding but lack clear positioning need to know who they're building for and why they're different — *before they waste their runway*. Traditional branding agencies are slow, generic, and disconnected from product. **MarcVista is a brand-and-product positioning sprint that gives founders clarity in 2 weeks by combining brand strategy with product direction** — unlike agencies that treat brand and product as separate problems.

**One-line:** MarcVista helps early-stage tech founders get crystal-clear on their positioning before they build — so they ship the right product to the right market, faster.

## Target customers (ICP)

**Segment 1 — Pre-product founders (just raised seed).** Technical founders turned entrepreneur, ex-big-tech PMs, solo founders, slightly-misaligned co-founder teams. They have **$500K–$1.2M** in seed and a general idea, but struggle to say who it's for or why someone would choose them. Burning runway without a north star. Budget **$3K–$8K** if the ROI is clear; decide in days-to-a-week when pain is acute. Discover via podcasts, X/Twitter, LinkedIn, founder communities.

**Segment 2 — Post-launch founders (weak positioning).** Live product but confusing/generic messaging; bootstrappers stuck at a ceiling; Series-A founders preparing to raise. They need positioning that shortens the sales cycle and reads clearly to investors.
