# Imagery

MarcVista's visual world is **abstract and geometric** — violet on near-white, sharp shapes, the angular mark motif. This was a deliberate choice: the brand sells clarity and engineering, not lifestyle. Imagery should feel designed, not stock.

## The single most important rule

**No stock people, no AI-generated humans, no generic "AI" clichés.** If a real photo is used, it must be a real person, client, or workspace from MarcVista's actual world — never a purchased "diverse team around a laptop."

## Primary direction — abstract / geometric

- **Violet geometric compositions:** angular shapes echoing the M/V mark, sharp diagonals, intersecting planes.
- **Soft single-hue washes:** radial `brand/100 → paper` (see `../assets/patterns/brand-wash.svg`). One gradient, subtle, never rainbow.
- **Dot grid / hairline structure:** the `grid.svg` pattern at low opacity for texture and "engineered" feel.
- **The mark as motif:** oversized, cropped, low-contrast (`brand/50`–`brand/100`) behind content as a watermark — sparingly.
- **Backgrounds from the site** (planet / abstract renders) are acceptable when they stay abstract and don't drift into generic "AI orb" territory.

## If photography is used (secondary)

- **What we'd shoot:** real founders/clients, real screens of shipped work, real workspaces. Candid over posed.
- **How:** natural light, honest framing, minimal retouching. Sharp crops (0px corners).
- **Treatment:** can sit under a violet duotone or a dark scrim when text overlays it.
- **Never:** stock-photo handshakes, headset call-center smiles, fake "team" shots, lens-flare hustle imagery.

## Illustration

- Style: flat, geometric, single-accent (violet) on paper — matching the mark's language.
- **Never:** 3D isometric, Memphis confetti, corporate-Memphis blob people, glossy gradients, claymorphism.

## Generic-AI clichés to never use

Glowing orbs · neural-mesh / node webs · wireframe globes · brains made of circuits · robot hands · binary rain · floating holographic UI · purple-blue "AI" gradients with sparkles. MarcVista is AI-native but never signals it with stock AI tropes.

## OG / social image (1200×630)

- Paper or `brand/900` background.
- Stacked or horizontal lockup top-left.
- One sharp headline (sentence case, display weight), max ~8 words.
- Optional: oversized mark motif cropped on the right at low contrast.
- One accent only — violet. No photo of people unless it's a real client case study.

## Patterns & backgrounds

- `assets/patterns/grid.svg` — 16px dot grid, `ink/200` dots on paper. Texture, low opacity.
- `assets/patterns/brand-wash.svg` — radial `brand/100 → paper`. Hero/section backdrop.
- Hairline rules (`line #E5E7EB`) to divide sections.
- **No** animated backgrounds, particle fields, aurora gradients, or video loops.

## Fallback order when no image exists

1. Abstract geometric composition (violet shapes on paper).
2. Brand-wash + the mark motif as a watermark.
3. Dot-grid pattern with a single headline.
4. Solid `brand/900` panel with white type.
Never reach for stock photography to "fill" a space — a clean type-and-shape layout always beats generic stock.
